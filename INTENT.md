# The Book of Sol Intent

The Book of Sol is Li Goldragon's publishable essay collection on solar nourishment, Ayurveda, chloride toxicology, yogic practice, cosmology, and the practical recovery of older science. The repo's purpose is to hold the finished prose surface: self-contained articles, the source extracts that support them, generated article images, and the metadata needed to publish the work cleanly.

The project speaks in a declarative, textually grounded voice. Classical witnesses carry the argument wherever possible, especially Sanskrit and Ayurvedic sources, and the prose avoids modern reductionist nutrition vocabulary when the older categories name the thing more accurately.

Publishing is local-first. Articles live as Markdown in category directories, source extracts stay separate under `source-extracts/`, and Substack state is tracked through front matter plus `.substack-posts.json`. Generated banners should embody the spirit of an article rather than mechanically illustrate a scene.

The collection is not a private notebook. It is the public-facing Book of Sol surface: a coherent body of doctrine, argument, and image prepared for readers.
