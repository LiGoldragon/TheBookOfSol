# The Vedas are the Superior Scriptures

First insight: There was a more advanced civilization Around 5000 years ago.

Second Insight: The Sanskrit Scriptures are, for various reasons, the most
intact written remnants of that civilization's wisdom.

Third Insight: Properly understood Ayurveda (with removed contents re-integrated
from oral Yogic traditions - ie Haṭha Yoga Pradīpikā) presents the highest
understanding of Terrestrial health.
