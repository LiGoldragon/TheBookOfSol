# Solar Excess

## Why More Fruit Cannot Fix a Fruitarian

The fruitarian's deficit is not solar. Fruit is solar — it clears, rises, disperses, refines. Śivāmbu is solar — it purifies, burns upward, strips away. The fruitarian has no shortage of the ascending force. The clearing is real. The lightness is real. The sharpness is real.

What is absent is the lunar.

Ghee descends. It binds. It coats. It builds ojas — the quiet, unctuous reserve that holds the organism together between clearings. Without it, the solar force refines into nothing. It burns through burden, then through reserve, then through tissue itself. This is not failure of the sun. It is absence of the moon.

Two solar forces do not produce a lunar effect. More fruit after fruit is more clearing after clearing. More śivāmbu after fruit is more sun after sun. The channels dry. Vāta rises. The organism, scoured and bright, begins to consume its own structure because nothing lunar arrived to rebuild what was stripped.

The correction is ghee. Not more fruit. Not more plasma. Not more fasting. Ghee — warm, heavy, binding, descending, lunar. The substance the Gītā calls snigdha. The substance the Pradīpikā names first among the yogin's foods. The substance that does what fruit cannot: stay.

One pillar clears. The other builds. Neither stands alone.
