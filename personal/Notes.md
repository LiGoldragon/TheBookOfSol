# The Vedas are the Superior Scriptures

First insight: There was a more advanced civilization around 5000 years ago.

Second insight: The Sanskrit Scriptures are, for various reasons, the most intact written remnants of that civilization's wisdom.

Third insight: Properly understood Āyurveda (with removed contents re-integrated from oral Yogic traditions, i.e. Haṭha Yoga Pradīpikā) presents the highest understanding of terrestrial health.
