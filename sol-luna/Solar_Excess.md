# Solar Excess

## Why More Fruit Cannot Fix a Fruitarian

The fruitarian's deficit is lunar. Fruit is solar — it clears, rises, disperses, refines. Śivāmbu (one's own water, the urine-elixir) is solar — it purifies, burns upward, strips away. The fruitarian has no shortage of the ascending force. The clearing is real. The lightness is real. The sharpness is real.

What is absent is the lunar.

Ghee descends. It binds. It coats. It builds ojas (the quiet, unctuous reserve that holds the organism together between clearings). Without it, the solar force refines into nothing. It burns through burden, then through reserve, then through tissue itself. The sun has not failed; the moon never arrived.

Two solar forces do not produce a lunar effect. More fruit after fruit is more clearing after clearing. More śivāmbu after fruit is more sun after sun. The channels dry. Vāta (the dry, mobile principle) rises. The organism, scoured and bright, begins to consume its own structure because nothing lunar arrived to rebuild what was stripped.

The correction is ghee, not more fruit, plasma, or fasting. Ghee — warm, heavy, binding, descending, lunar. The substance the Gītā calls snigdha (unctuous, oily). The substance the Pradīpikā names first among the yogin's foods. The substance that does what fruit cannot: stay.

One pillar clears. The other builds. Neither stands alone.
